Team 4th Dimension are a group of programming enthusiasts who met in a class at KU. Though originally they began working together for a class project, they found that they worked well with one another and got along well. The majority of the team went on to work on the next class project which evolved past simply an assignment and and is currently being developed into the Android application "LoL Helper".

Team Builder

Team Builder



A LoL Helper exclusive, Team Builder will help you choose champions that work well together. Enter a couple champions that have already been selected and have it recommend who else would be a good pick, or recommend a playstyle.


Player Statistics

Player Statistics



Enter a summoner name and view their stats, including statistics for temporary game modes such as One For All and U.R.F.


Counters

Counters



Look up who counters and is countered by a champion, so you can have an advantage over your opponent.


Ultimate Bravery

Ultimate Bravery



Click the button to be given a random champion and a random build for a fun game mode.


Build Guides

Build Guides



Not sure what items to buy on a new champion? Consult our custom build guides for help.


Jungle Timers

Jungle Timers



Keep track of when jungle monsters will spawn. LoL Helper gives you audio and visual cues for each camp as spawn time draws nearer.


General Info

General Info



Look up detailed information on champions, items, and summoner spells so that you can plan strategies for your next game.
